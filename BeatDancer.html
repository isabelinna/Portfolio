Beat Dancer

"Beat Dancer" is a a project which involves the creation of a platformer video game that aims to foster user interest in percussion instruments through an interactive story where they can learn more about them. This project was developed using unity with c#, this game showcases seamless gameplay mechanics and immersive visuals that bring the vibrant worlds to life. In this project, I took on the task of designing and creating captivating animations for sprites of various characters and objects. Through meticulous attention to detail and creative expertise, I brought these sprites to life, ensuring smooth and engaging visual experiences.

In adition, we also developed a website to support the game. This website provides players with a user manual and a download link, making it easy for them to get started with the game. We also implemented a dashboard for the game administrators, which allows them to track player statistics from all around the world.

Here is a plot summary:

In this captivating project, let me introduce you to Katsby, a music-loving cat who one day falls asleep and finds himself transported to different worlds in his dreams. In these mesmerizing dreamscapes, Katsby encounters a group of talented mice who are skilled percussionists. Catsby must cross from lush tropical jungles to icy landscapes, each setting offers an opportunity to explore and discover the diverse sounds of percussion.

As Katsby embarks on this dreamlike journey, players have the chance to learn about different types of percussion instruments through interactive interactions and exciting musical challenges. From tribal drums to vibrant marimbas, each instrument has its own charm and distinctive sound, allowing players to experience their magic as they progress through the story.

With this game, users will learn:

  • Percussion instruments in an interactive and fun way
  • Safety measures needed for playing percussion instruments
  • The difference between instruments
  • Fun facts
  • The importance and benefits of having the ability to play an instrument
  • The value of friendship
