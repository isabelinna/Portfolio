Atizappan

"Atizappan" is a powerful and user-friendly mobile application designed to enhance civil security and public safety in Atizapán de Zaragoza. With Atizappan, residents can stay informed and up-to-date with the latest national news and events in real-time. The app features a unique alert notification system that sends push notifications to users in the event of emergencies, such as earthquakes, fires, and floods, keeping them informed and prepared. Additionally, Atizappan includes an emergency number directory that provides quick access to important contact information in case of emergencies. Developed with a user-centric approach, Atizappan is the perfect solution for Atizapán de Zaragoza residents who want to stay informed and connected to their community, while keeping themselves and their families safe and secure.

As part of my contribution to Atizappan, I designed and implemented the news and alerts interfaces using APIs (Application Programming Interfaces). These features enable users to stay up-to-date with the latest national news relevant to civil security, as well as receive real-time notifications from the website we created for emergency events in Atizapán de Zaragoza.

Additionally, as part of our comprehensive approach to civil security, we developed a dedicated website for the Civil Protection Department. This website serves as a central platform for authorized personnel to log in and send important alerts and notifications directly to residents who have installed the Atizappan app.
